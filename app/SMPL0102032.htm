Video Template - FEMA Media Library

This page contains an example of a video. Videos should be in MP4 format and must use video.dwt template. The MediaElement.js player is used to show the video in a compatible format based upon the user's browser. The video must also contain both Closed Captioning (.srt file) and a transcript.

This page also contains an example of an internal link. Internal links can be used for various reasons including linking to additional content, printable materials, and transcripts.
