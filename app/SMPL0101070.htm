Gather Materials

Before we begin, gather all necessary materials and ensure that you have the proper programs installed on your computer. Materials include:

  • EMI Course Template (V3.0) Zipped
  • DreamWeaver Snippets (Extension) - Optional code snippets. You may choose to either use these snippets with DreamWeaver or use the provided code as a template for your new course.
  • HTML editor of your choice (Example: DreamWeaver).

Note: This guide discusses the use of Adobe DreamWeaver and DreamWeaver Snippets. DreamWeaver is not a requirement. You may use another HTML editor.
