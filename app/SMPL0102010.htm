Lesson 2: Lesson Overview

This lesson presents examples of different types of page content that may be used in your Independent Study course.

Objectives: At the end of this lesson, you should be able to:

  • Create a page containing a video in the correct format.
  • Create pages for each of the following types of Knowledge Checks: True/False, Checkbox/Multiple Select, Text Areas, Ordering, Multiple Choice, Yes/No Radio buttons
  • Utilize appropriate layouts while developing various types of content including: basic text, internal links, external links, lists, images, and interactive content.

This lesson should take approximately 30 minutes to complete.
