Take the Exam

Congratulations for having completed the final lesson. To receive credit for this course, you must take the final exam and answer 75% of the questions correctly. To take the exam:

  • Click this link to print these instructions.
  • Go to Learning Center
  • Go to Tests
  • Type the Course Number, e.g. IS-105, in the Search box
  • Click the Search button

The test link will then be displayed. When you are ready, click the link and the test will launch. Ensure you click the Continued button on the last page of the test.

You can then visit My Transcript and look for your course completion. If you were not successful on the first test attempt, don’t worry, you can take it again!

Good Luck!
